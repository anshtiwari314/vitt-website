About Vitt.ai

Answers your question -Now you have a financial advisor at your fingertips - have any financial question or doubt, just fire away the query to Finity and she’ll give a prompt and smart answer. She is your go-to person for financial queries.

Unbiased

Vitt is a robot and follows set algorithms. She will provide the best advice that is suitable for your profile. If a stock investment is wrong for you, she will suggest a safer investment option. Now investors have no reason to be afraid or duped.

5-star recommendation

Vitt’s algorithms have been built and tested over a long arduous period of 2 years. There are various inputs that go into the recommendations, including long-term data (core economic data points) and short-term data (a news item that is critical toward a certain industry or stock price).

Simplified

All tough to tackle financial concepts are explained in easy-to-digest tidbits, because that’s what finance is. It’s very easy to understand. Now you can fire away questions at will without dreading what’s going to come.
